# Winner-Takes-All Multivariate Probabilistic Forecasting

**Cortes et al. (2025)**

## Summary (3 bullets)

- Proposes a winner-takes-all (WTA) training objective for multivariate probabilistic forecasting that improves diversity of the predictive distribution by assigning training signal only to the best-performing sample in each batch.
- Addresses mode collapse in multivariate probabilistic forecasters; applicable on top of existing backbone architectures.
- **Applicability to P1**: low priority; relevant if P1 moves to probabilistic multivariate outputs, but not critical for current backbone selection.
